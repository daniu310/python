### 2.安装heroku命令工具
- 建立描述文件
    - runtime.txt->python-3.6.4
    - requirements.txt-> Flask  gunicorn
    - Procfile->web   gunicorn main:app

- heroku安装
    - [heroku cli](https://devcenter.heroku.com/articles/heroku-cli)
    - 登陆 ```bash
heroku login```

 cd my-project/
